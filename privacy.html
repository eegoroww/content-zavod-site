This Privacy Policy describes how Content Zavod ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use our social media content distribution platform and related services (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

When you register for and use Content Zavod, we may collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and login credentials for Content Zavod.
  • Social Media Account Credentials: OAuth tokens and API credentials necessary to connect your social media accounts (YouTube, VK, TikTok, Instagram) to our Service. We use industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocols and do not store your social media passwords.
  • Content Data: Video files, descriptions, titles, hashtags, and other metadata that you upload or create through the Service for distribution to social media platforms.
  • Cloud Storage Credentials: Access tokens for cloud storage services (such as Yandex Disk) that you connect to manage your video content library.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Service, we may automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the Service, including features used, actions taken, posting schedules configured, and content categories selected.
  • Log Data: Technical information including posting success/failure status, timestamps, error messages, and platform response data necessary for service operation and troubleshooting.
  • Device Information: Operating system type and version for compatibility and support purposes.

1.3 Information from Third-Party Platforms

When you connect your social media accounts, we may receive limited information from those platforms as permitted by their APIs, including:

  • Account identifiers and channel/page names
  • Upload status and video identifiers for content we post on your behalf
  • API quota and rate limit information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To upload and publish your video content to the social media platforms you have authorized, at the times you have scheduled.
  • Content Management: To access your cloud storage to retrieve video files you have designated for posting.
  • Description Generation: To generate unique descriptions for your content based on templates and preferences you configure.
  • Scheduling: To manage your posting calendar and execute content distribution according to your plan.
  • Logging and Monitoring: To track posting status, record successes and failures, and provide you with activity reports.
  • Service Improvement: To understand usage patterns and improve the Service functionality.
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and resolve technical issues you may encounter.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Social Media Platforms: We transmit your video content and associated metadata (titles, descriptions, hashtags) to the social media platforms (YouTube, VK, TikTok, Instagram) that you have explicitly authorized and connected to your account. This sharing is essential to provide the core functionality of the Service.
  • Cloud Storage Providers: We access your cloud storage (e.g., Yandex Disk) solely to retrieve video files you have designated for posting.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose information when we believe it is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • OAuth tokens and API credentials are stored locally on your device in encrypted configuration files.
  • Video content is stored in your own cloud storage account (e.g., Yandex Disk) and is only temporarily cached on your local device during the upload process. Temporary cached files are automatically deleted after successful posting.
  • Posting logs and schedule data are stored locally on your device.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Use of OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication with third-party platforms (we never store your social media passwords)
  • Local storage of sensitive credentials on your own device rather than on remote servers
  • Automatic cleanup of temporary video files after posting
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all API communications with social media platforms and cloud storage services

5. Data Retention

  • OAuth Tokens: Stored until you disconnect the associated social media account or revoke access.
  • Posting Logs: Retained for 30 days for troubleshooting and reporting purposes, then automatically deleted.
  • Cached Video Files: Automatically deleted immediately after successful upload to social media platforms.
  • Schedule and Plan Data: Retained until you create a new plan or manually delete the data.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can access all your data at any time, as it is stored locally on your device.
  • Deletion: You can delete any or all of your data at any time by removing the local application files, tokens, and logs.
  • Revoke Platform Access: You can disconnect any social media platform at any time by revoking OAuth access through that platform's settings or by deleting the stored tokens.
  • Data Portability: Your data is stored in standard formats (JSON, YAML) and can be easily exported.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can stop using the Service at any time and delete all associated data from your device.
